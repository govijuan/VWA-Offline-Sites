  • How to help the municipality so that all the garbage is actually taken to the official dumping ground?
    Give us ideas and work with us, so the garbage is just in one place.
  • How to get a recycling system for Vrindavan’s garbage?
    This is a good question… Teach us ways to recycle for Vrindavan!
  • How to organize the dumping ground on the most efficient way?
    Here we can put all the garbage… Give solution for its best use!

    Find and give answers about this question!
  • What will happen to the switch water of Vrindavan?

YAMUNA DEVI

  • How to bring Yamuna ji back to the ghats?
    There is a big campaign to recover Yamunaji. Join us!
  • How to cleanse the water of the Yamuna?
    Yamuna River is suffering because of pollution, help us to clean!
  • How to stop inorganic materials from being thrown into the Yamuna?
    Protect our Sacred River… Don’t litter!

PROTECT HEALTHY VALUES

  • How to get a sand paths on Vrindavan’s parikrama marg?
    As Sri Krishna walked on this sand, it is sacred … help us to build lots of sand paths along the Vrindavan Parikrama marg, all the route around the Dham.
  • How to stop the selling of meat and fish and eggs in Vrindavan?
    Replace meat, fish and eggs, with healthy food and spread its consume all over Vrindavan.

TRAFFIC

  • How to keep the sound of the trumpet speakers down so that neighbors are not disturbed?
    Trumpet speakers may be noisy and cause disturbances. Rights are related with duties towards others, including aged, sick and quietly neighbors, too.
  • How to stop bicycles and scooters from racing on Vrindavan’s narrow gauge tracks?
    There are many other places in the world where people can run a race, so why are they using the narrow gauge tracks around the sacred Dham to do it?Why don’t they take care of children, old people, pilgrims, cows and other animals and vehicles?
  • How to organize the traffic in Vrindavan to avoid traffic jams?
    The Dham needs to find the way to organize its traffic better in order to make easier the passing through the streets of so many rickshaws, bicycles, scooters and carts.

EDUCATION

  • How to educate school-age children times to not throw garbage into the street?
    Educating children in the schools of Vrindavan having in mind environmental issues is the starting point.Education should start making children aware about not to throw garbage into the streets and the big problems of sewerage and waste disposal linked to that.
  • How to provide adequate education to the poorest section of Vrindavan?
    To educate poor people is a priority because it is linked to the way we can tackle one of the major problems: to protect them and provide better health care and sanitary rehabilitation.

REFORESTATION

  • How to increase the forest cover in Vrindavan by creating more green areas, especially on the other side of the Yamuna?
    • Sages dwelt in these forests, living simple and austere lives in search of spiritual perfection. So, try to device ways to plant new trees and protect the old ones.Help the India’s Forest Department to improve the reforestation and protection of the green belt which runs between Vrindavan and Mathura is absolutely necessary

COW PROTECTION

  • How to protect the cows of Braj?
    The Vedic culture taught that the earth and the cow are to be loved and cared for as mothers, so it is necessary to devise ways to protect and defend them.
  • How to avoid nighttime cattle rustling and slaughter in Braj Mandal?
    Sri Krishna played his flute and herded cows in the forest of Braj, so they must be protected from slaughter, rustling and other kinds of violence at the hands of humans.

  • Who is providing health care in Vrindavan and in what conditions?
    Health care should be the responsibility of the authorities. Are they the office of the Sanitary Inspector and the Health Officer of Vrindavan Municipal Council? Who can tell us?

 

If they have limited sources of income it will be great to help them by starting health projects, such as providing the Dham with better basic sanitation services.

  • Where to create peacock sanctuaries along the parikrama?
    The peacocks used to dance in great ecstasy in front of Sri Krishna, and they only spend the night in the higher and bigger trees. Now, many ancient trees have been cut down, so would you suggest us where to build sanctuaries to shelter the peacocks, please?
  • How to handle Vrindavan’s the monkey population?
    The overpopulation of monkeys is certainly not confined to jungles, they are cute but sometimes no sweet and gentle with tourists and they represent a constant menace to the newly planted trees. Please if you have any ideas to solve this, tell us.
  • Who are the pioneers in Vrindavan who are exemplary in making improvements in Vrindavan?
    Inform us about those who has been working dynamically on many issues and have initiated pioneering enterprises to enhance, protect and preserve the Dham.

I live in Switzerland. This place is so full of natural beauty, and I always think" This is what Vrindavan shold be like" The mountains remind me of Giriraj, the lakes of the lakes near Govardhan.The government and the people have maintained this country so beautifully. Our Vrindavan should be even better than this. The way Sri Vrindavan is described in our scriptures is so beautiful, yet today as the day go by, all we see is Sri Vrindavan being turned into a concrete jungle. What are we going to show our children? They grow up reading such beautiful descriptions of Sr Vrindavan, and then what meets their eyes?

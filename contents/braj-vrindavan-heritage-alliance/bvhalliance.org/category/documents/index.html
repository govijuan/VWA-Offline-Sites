June 24th, 2013Chief secy: Free UP river beds of encroachments – Times of India 24th June 2013

Share

In the aftermath of the large-scale natural calamity in Uttarakhand, the UP government has finally woken up and asked Ghaziabad and Gautam Budh Nagar administrations, along with other districts, to submit status report on the river bed zone. The UP chief secretary held a meeting with senior officers and also directed the development authorities to ensure that flood zones are free from encroachments.

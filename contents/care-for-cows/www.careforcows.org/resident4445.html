Krsna

Is a Bull, born on 2006-02-19, who joined us on 2007-01-18.

I was born and raised in Govardhana. I'm really easy going and maybe even a little slow. You see, I never did get quite enough to eat when I was little. Back in August of 2006, my body had grown too lanky and long for my skinny, wobbly legs to hold. All a cow would have to do is touch me for my back legs to fall sideways causing me to crumple to the ground. One day, I stumbled on Balarama who was having three square meals a day from a family living in Govardhana. I decided I wanted some too. When the lady there started brushing me, I was convinced. This was the place to be. So I settled in with Balarama. When he ate out of his bowl, I shared with him. When he took a nap, I snoozed too. When the lady brushed him, I shoved my nose in the way so I could get some too. And when Balarama wandered around eating grass I happily munched it too. The grass dried up some time around Kartika, so I followed Balarama into the streets. It was rough out there. I got really dirty and often I had a stomach ache from eating filthy, rotten food. But I'm really simple. If Bala does it, I do it too. One day we got too close to a fruit cart. The infuriated man struck my eye with such force that it remained swollen shut for two weeks. The family that had been feeding us decided that we'd be much safer in Vrndavan at Care for Cows. We're boys, you know. And it's only a question of time before someone decides that we are worth more dead than alive. So here we are at Care for Cows. My eye is still fogged over which is annoying because I can't see too well out of it. But they say it will get better soon. And boy does a full tummy sit well with me. I'm perfectly content here. Oh, and did I mention that I really really love gur?

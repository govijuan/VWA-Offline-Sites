resident

Sudevi

Is a Cow, born on 1997-01-23, who joined us on 2002-08-25.

My former owner mistreated me so I took a vow never to let him milk me. Owing to that he decided to turn me out. I witnessed as he lied to the Care for Cows men telling them that I was gentle and gave eight liters of milk daily. When they tried to milk me the next morning, I was impossible and defeated them easily. But they were very patient and treated me with respect and gradually I became fond of them. When I gave birth to Nitai, I let them milk me without any fuss. This is the proof that I am happy here. If people would just treat us with respect, we will happily provide them with all they need for a simple and happy life.

Sudevi is sponsored by Frank & Connie

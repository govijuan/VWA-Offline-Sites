Belvan

Is a Bull, born on 2006-11-16, who joined us on 2006-11-27.

I was born in Vrindavan dhama, on the auspicious day of ekadasi, right at Care for Cows, where my future is secure. Hence I must be some great soul, though I don't let on about that and try to act very carefree. My mother is a strong black cow named Bhumi who is very protective of me and gives me lots of very good milk. I have an elder brother and sister here too. I'm named Belvan after a forest on the bank of the Yamuna River.

Once upon a time, in the school of a small town lived a teacher call Sudama. He uses to teach how to live an honest and holy life. Nevertheless Sudama had no money. Those his family sometimes got very little to eat. Their cloth was old and with holes.

Sudama's wife was worry about the health of the family, and one day she asks him: Your old friend Gopal, has become a king, and we are starving of hunger, why you not go and ask him to help us. I am sure he will.

Sudama told her: "I have not seeing him since many years, and I will not come now to ask him for money. Nevertheless it would be nice to meet again my most dear friend Gopal Krishna.

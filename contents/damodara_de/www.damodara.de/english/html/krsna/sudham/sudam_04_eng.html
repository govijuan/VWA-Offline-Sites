While walking through the woods, Sudama remembered something very special when his master of school send them -him and Gopal- to bring wood to make a fire.

That day had being a very hot day. While they collected the woods, the boys played in the soft grasses of the forest or swim in the cooling waters of the lakes they found on the way.

Waiving in the braches of the trees, they imitated the monkeys of the forest. Both - Sudam and Gopal- enjoyed throwing the leaves of the trees in the air, while they watch how the wind blew them in all directions.

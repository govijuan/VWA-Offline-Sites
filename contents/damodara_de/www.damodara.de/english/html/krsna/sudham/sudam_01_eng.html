From ancient India, come the mystic and enchanting histories of Gopal the cow herder. Living the life of a simple village boy, he is actually God himself exhibiting his sweetest aspect, because in Vrindavan, where he lives, he forgets his position as God, the Supreme Being, the creator and controller of everything, to enjoy the love of his devotees.

His adventures in the forest of Vrindavan are a fountain of teachings, joy and happiness to those who hear them. Endowed with unlimited powers, God shows again and again that his strongest power is the love for his surrendered devotees, for his friends, and all souls...

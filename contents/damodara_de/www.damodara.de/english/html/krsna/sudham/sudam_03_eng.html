Sudam told her: "Ok, I will visit him, but I can't go empty-handed, is there anything at home that I could give him? We don't have anything, but our neighbors could give us something for him, she says.

She them went to ask them for help, but they were very simple people also, they did not had that much, only a punch of broken rice, which she took in a little bag. She gave them then to Sudam saying: "Is only a bit of broken rice, but that's all we can give..." Saying good-bye to his family and taking the bag, Sudam left heading to the magnificent city of Dwarka, the city of Gopal Krishna, his old friend...

At the same time in the school, Sandipani Muni the teacher, was very much worry about them. "Why is that they have not come? Probably because of the storm? I hope they are fine. Soon he send a team to search after them. They search late in the night but they could not fine them. Until the next morning they found them sleeping under a bush.

Sandipani was very happy to see them again, he told them: "I see that you had a lot of trouble collecting the wood for the school. And even with that strong storm you have collect so much. You have work very hard. I bless you my little boys, that you will remain as good friends, and that you will always have what you need."

While remembering all this, Sudam was very happy. He enjoy the journey through the beautiful forest along the way, until he finally reach Dwaraka, the city of Gopal Krishna, the king, his old friend...

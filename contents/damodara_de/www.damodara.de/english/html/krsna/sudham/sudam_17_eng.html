When the waves smashed against the ground, it produces a very pleasing sound, and the place was a very peaceful one to make the nest. One day, the winds were stronger and the clouds covered the sunshine. The waves rose with tremendous strength, threatening the see shore. While all this was happening the sparrow was frightened, but she could not move the nest without hurting the eggs.

Flying upon the waves she scream crying: "Oh great ocean, please do not come with your waves at this side of the see shore, here is my nest, and my little babies are there. Please do not take them away from me!"

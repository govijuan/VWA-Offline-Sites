Sudam felt like being in the wrong place, at the wrong time. Every palace he met was greater than the previous one. Until he reach an amazing construction in pure white marble with golden decorations and jewels incrusted in the walls. He asked the doorkeeper who was living in that palace. "The King and queen of Dwaraka", says the doorkeeper. Sudam started looking at his cloth and dirty feet after the long journey. "I can't see the king like this, I can't go inside so dirty. I think I better go back. What am I doing here? The king should be very busy.

While leaving the palace, he suddenly heard: "Sudam! It's me! Your Gopal, please don't go! Sudam could not believe... With a big hug, both friends started crying.

After 2 months of journey, when he arrived at the village, he thought: "Am I in the wrong village? There were fruit trees everywhere, lakes and flowers, and all the houses where beautiful and nicely decorated. In the place where his house originally was, was now a red marble palace, more amazing that any of the palaces he sow in Dwaraka. He could not recognize his wife, dressed so nicely; she also did not looked weak and sick. She was there, looking like a queen, with a beautiful smile as she always used, at the door of the house, calling the children to received Sudam.

Sudam could understand: "Gopal is behind all this, how incredible, he has give us so much, and I did not even ask him for anything, I only gave him that bag of broken rice..."

One day Gopal and his friends where playing in the forest. A mighty and terrible demon named Agha watched them. He hated anyone who was happy. And he liked to give troubles to others out of malice. So he decided to kill all of them. This demon was so terrible that all powerful warriors were afraid of him. As he watched he thought, “I will kill this Gopal, and all his friends and calves. With the children dead all the people of Vrindaban will die of grief!”
Then Agha grew longer and longer until he was eight miles long. He stretched his mouth wider and wider until it was as a mountain cave. His sharp teeth appeared like peaks on that mountain, and his hot tongue appeared like a broad road. His eyes blazed fire. Agha decided to shallow all of them at once, so he stayed on the path waiting.

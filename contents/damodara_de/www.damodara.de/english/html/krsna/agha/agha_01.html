From Ancient India come the mystical and enchanting stories of Gopal, the cowherd boy . While living the life of a simple village boy, Gopal is actually one of Visnu’s encarnations. His adventures in the forest of Vrindavan bring both joy and amazement to those who hear them. Endowed with limitless powers and riches, Gopal shows time and time again, that His greatest strength is His love and affection for his friends, family, cows and all living entities.

Long ago in a place call Vrindaban, Gopal Krishna sported with his cowherd boyfriends, just like an ordinary child.

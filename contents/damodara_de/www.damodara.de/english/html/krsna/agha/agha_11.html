Suddenly, Agha’s glittering spirit soul merged into the body of Gopal. All the celestial beings were overwhelmed with joy. Showering flowers on Gopal, they dance, beat drums and sang songs. “Jay, Jay, all glories to Gopal.

The gigantic, fierce mouth of the demon stayed open for many days. Slowly his body dried up and became a stone which can be seen even today in India.

Later the Gopas used it as a playground.

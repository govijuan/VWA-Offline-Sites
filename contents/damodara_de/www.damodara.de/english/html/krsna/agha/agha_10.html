Once inside the demon throat, Gopal grew bigger and bigger. Agha chocked, thrashed about, and his big eyes moved violently. His life air burst from a hole in the top of his head and waited in the sky. It was like a dazzling light that spread everywhere.
Inside Agha’s belly, Krishna found all of his friends unconscious. With his sweet glance, he brought them all to life. Then they marched right out of the demon’s mouth.

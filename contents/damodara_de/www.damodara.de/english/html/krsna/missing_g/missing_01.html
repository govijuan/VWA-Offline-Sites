From Ancient India come the mystical and enchanting stories of Gopal, the cowherder. While living the life of a simple village boy, Gopal is actually God himself playing human. His adventures in the forest of Vrindavan bring both joy and amazement to those who hear them. Endowed with limitless powers and riches, Gopal shows time and time again his greatest strength is his love and affection for his friends, family, cows and all.

Gopal told Brahma, “Each day I go to the pasturing grounds with my friends and calves. If you know where they might be, please tell me.”

“You have not lost your friends,” Brahma said. “I tried to steal them from you. But I could not trick you. You are the Supreme Lord. Everything comes from you. You have shown me that you can become everything. But still you stay different from everything. Please forgive me for being so foolish.”

Brahma returned to his own planet. He would never again try to test Gopal’s mystic powers.

The mothers of Vrindavan Village heard the boys’ flutes. They ran out to embrace their sons, just as they always did. Each mother took her son on her lap and hugged him tightly.

Gopal, in the form of each of the cowherd boys, accepted this offering of love from all the mothers.

“It is usually Gopal who holds a special place in my heart,” one mother told her son as she gave him a kiss. “Perhaps it is because of all the wonderful things he does. But tonight I feel as if you are stealing that special place in my heart.”

“I will show Brahma who is the master of all Mystics,” Gopal said.

He then turned himself into many, many boys. Gopal also changed himself into many, many calves. Each boy and each calf looked and acted just like one that Brahma had hidden.

The boys played on their flutes, just as they always did. The calves’ hooves raised a golden cloud of dust, just as they always did. The boys put the calves into their cowsheds before they went home, just as they always did.

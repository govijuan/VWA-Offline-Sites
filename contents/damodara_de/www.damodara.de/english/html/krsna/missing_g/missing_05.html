Brahma, who is in charge of many things in this world, was very powerful. He had so many things to think of, and so many things to create that he needed four heads!

One day when Brahma thought Gopal was not watching, he led all of the Gopal’s calves to a secret cave. He waved his four arms and put the calves into a magical sleep.

Then when Gopal left his friends to search for the missing calves, Brahma made all the cowherd boys go to the same cave. Under Brahma’s magical spell, they also fell soundly asleep.

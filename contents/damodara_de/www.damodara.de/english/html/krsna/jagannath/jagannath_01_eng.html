The Apearance of Lord Jagannath
By Somath Kuntya - Pujari of the Jagannath Temple


On the festival Ratha Yatra, the procession of the cars of Lord Jagannatha, Subhadra and Balarama is celebrated each year in India in the city Puri. The Lord is worshipped in one of the biggest temples in the world. The history of how Lord Jagannatha appeared is a very interesting episode of Vedic Culture.
